How you can help other farmers:

On this trip we've been lucky enough to get help from a group called the Suidlanders (or Southlanders). They are a civil defence force preparing for peaceful aid and protection of South Africa's minority Afrikaner population if any sort of civil war or serious government persecution breaks out. You can check out their website and support them at https://suidlanders.org/
